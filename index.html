YouCast

Turn YouTube Channels into Subscribable Podcasts

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

YouCast allows you to subscribe to video feeds on YouTube as podcasts in any standard podcatcher like iTunes, BeyondPod on Android or even Zune PC and so forth. You can also sort videos by popularity for these channels you know you will never finish.

Features

  1. Subscribe to any channel's public video uploads.
  2. Subscribe to any public playlist on YouTube.
  3. Get audio-only feeds (for faster downloads).
  4. Sort the videos by popularity (awesome for huge channels, like TED Talks)
  5. Run YouCast at home and access it from everywhere on the internet, not just your home network.
  6. Limit the amount of videos in any feed (helps with users with thousands of videos).
  7. Use YouCast from any device in your network: iPhone, Android, Tablet. (Just open the incoming port 22703 on your firewall and paste the URL in your favorite podcatcher)

Usage

To get a URL for a podcast:

  1. Open YouCast
  2. Enter a YouTube channel's username or playlist ID.
  3. Choose a preferred quality (the better the quality the bigger the file).
  4. Generate and copy the podcast URL.
  5. Paste the URL in your favorite Podcatcher.
  6. While your podcatcher is updating podcasts YouCast must be running.
